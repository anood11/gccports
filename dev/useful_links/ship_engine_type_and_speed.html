G Gas Turbine
M Diesel
S Steam Turbine

Ships speed is expressed with "section". The ships speed varies with the type of ship. For instance, the speed of dry cargo ships and tankers is slow, which is usually 13 sections to 17 sections. And the speed of container ships is fast, which can reach 24.5 sections at most presently. The speed of passenger ship is also fast.
